Cosmetic Dentist & Aesthetic Practitioner

Dr. Othman Salih

BDS · GDC 170340 · Associate Dentist Since 2018 · Facial Aesthetic Specialist · Marlow

Othman began his career in dentistry by earning a Dental Technician Diploma in 1993, graduating at the top of his class. His passion for clinical dentistry led him to complete his BDS degree in 1996 — establishing a strong foundation for his future clinical work.

He holds a Postgraduate Certificate in Aesthetic & Restorative Dental Practice from the University of London / Eastman Dental Institute (2016) and a Postgraduate Certificate in Dental Implantology from VSS Academy, London (2020). In 2021 he further advanced his expertise with training in Advanced Facial Aesthetics, including Botox and dermal fillers for both cosmetic and medical purposes. He is also trained in providing sedation for anxious patients, ensuring a calm and reassuring experience for those who may feel nervous about dental treatment.

Othman has a strong clinical interest in Cosmetic Dentistry, comprehensive Invisalign orthodontic treatment, Dental Implants, Oral Surgery, and Facial Aesthetics. He takes great pride in his work, and his dedication to delivering high-quality, patient-centred care is reflected in the positive feedback he consistently receives from his patients.

With a perfect 5.0 rating across all Google reviews, patients consistently praise his painless technique, calm manner, and beautifully natural outcomes. He is currently in the final stages of completing a Diploma in Oral Surgery with DentHub Smiles.
